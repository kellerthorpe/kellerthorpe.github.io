Events

Tea and company go together, so please join us in one of our frequent social events.

Book Club

First and third Tuesday at 1pm

Every two weeks, we gather to discuss a different book related to tea or other culinary ventures. The next book is The Traveling Tea Shop by Belinda Jones. Feel free to attend even if you have not finished the book.

Knitting Circle

Every Thursday at 2pm

We gather weekly to have a cup of tea and knit, crochet, or engage in any other portable fiber art.

Tea and talk

Second Monday at 11am

Enjoy a cup of tea while participating in a discussion circle. Each month we introduce a new topic. Our theme this month is beginning a new garden.

Tea tasting

Fourth Saturday at 10am

Our monthly tea tasting is a wonderful opportunity to sample a variety of teas and discover new flavors. Reservations are recommended. (Please visit our reservations page.)
